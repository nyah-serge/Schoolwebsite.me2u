Mission Statement

The mission statement is building leaders of integrity to meet up with the challenges of globalization.

Queensway schools is fully accredited and authorised by the Ministry of Basic Education with the government of Cameroon. It is a cultural environment in which each child is inspired and empowered to reach his or her full academic, physical and emotional potentials to meet with the challenges of globalisation. Therefore the desired outcome is to educate the whole person i.e mind, body and will; hence the motto ‘Building Leaders of Integrity’.

Historical facts

Q.B.S and Q.I.C were created respectively in 1994 and 2005, was the brain child of Miss Playne Fuondjing a professional nurse and midwife for 31 years. She had this burning desire to nurture these children in their academic development.
